What is StockDash?

A personal stock/portfolio watchlist with price alerts, built around mortgage-adjacent tickers plus the 10Y Treasury and 15Y/30Y mortgage rates.

Where do prices come from?

Live quotes are pulled from Yahoo Finance and refresh automatically every 60 seconds while this tab is open. Rates come from the St. Louis Fed (FRED).

How do alerts work?

Every signed-in user tracking RKT automatically gets email alerts at ±6.5% and ±10% day moves — no configuration needed and no login required to receive them. On top of that, each tracked symbol (RKT or otherwise) can have an optional price-high, price-low, and/or % day-change thresholds. % day-change accepts a comma-separated list (e.g. 1, 3, 5, 10) so each move size fires its own alert. A background check runs every 15 minutes during US market hours; you get an email the moment a threshold is newly crossed, not repeatedly while it holds. All of these reset at the next trading day.

What's a one-time alert?

A price target that fires exactly once and stays dormant afterwards — it doesn't reset overnight. Use it for "email me when RKT hits $50" style triggers you don't want re-firing every day the condition holds. Configure them in the One-time alerts panel below the watchlist.

Who can see this?

Anyone signed in through Cloudflare Access. Each signed-in user has their own private watchlist, thresholds, and alert history — you only see and get emailed about your own.

What do Columns / Compact / Chart / theme do?

Display preferences saved in this browser only (not tied to your account): show or hide table columns, switch to a denser compact layout, overlay symbols' price history on a chart, or toggle light/dark.

What are the scrolling ticker rows?

Two continuously-scrolling tickers: trending housing/fintech/AI/Detroit headlines, and trending housing/Fed/economy prediction markets from Polymarket (public data, no account needed). Use the ⏸/▶ and ✕/👁 buttons on each to pause or hide it — both preferences are saved in this browser.
